# MIS Authenticator - User Guide

## Introduction

Welcome to the **MIS Authenticator**. This application serves as your secure digital key for accessing the MIS System ecosystem. By linking your device to your account, you can log in securely and rapidly without remembering complex passwords.

## Getting Started

### 1. Installation

- **Mobile (iOS/Android)**: Download "MIS Authenticator" from your organization's app catalog or the public App Store/Play Store (if available).
- **Web (PWA)**: Navigate to the authenticator URL in your browser and select "Add to Home Screen" to install it as an app.

### 2. First-Time Setup

1. **Launch the App**: Open the app on your device.
2. **Welcome Screen**: You will see the "MIS Authenticator" branding. Tap **Get Started**.
3. **Permissions**: Grant Camera permissions when prompted. This is required for scanning login QR codes.
4. **Link Account**: You will be asked to link your account.
    - Tap **Link Account**.
    - Scan the "Setup QR Code" provided on your MIS Profile page in the web portal.
    - Once verified, your device is securely linked.

## Using the App

### Logging In

1. Go to the MIS Portal login page on your computer.
2. Select **"Login with Authenticator"**.
3. A QR code will appear on the screen.
4. Open the **MIS Authenticator** app on your phone.
5. Tap the **Scan** tab (if not already open).
6. Point your camera at the screen.
7. The app will vibrate and confirm successful authentication. The web portal will automatically log you in.

### Viewing History

Tap the **History** tab to view a log of your recent authentication attempts. This helps you track your account activity and identify any suspicious access.

### Settings

In the **Settings** tab, you can:

- **Appearance**: Toggle between **Light** and **Dark** modes to suit your preference.
- **Clear History**: Remove your local scan logs (this does not affect the server logs).
- **Sign Out**: Unlink your device (requires re-linking to use again).

## Troubleshooting

- **Camera not working?** Go to your device Settings -> Privacy -> Camera and ensure MIS Authenticator has permission.
- **Scan fails?** Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
- **"Invalid Key" error?** Your device link may have expired. Go to Settings -> Sign Out, then re-link your account from the MIS Profile page.
